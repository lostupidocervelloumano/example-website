The Game

Enter the Labyrinth.
Claim the Arcane Throne.

Mage's Labyrinth is a competitive dungeon-crawl game where two to five players navigate treacherous corridors, cast powerful spells, and outwit ancient guardians in a race to claim the legendary Arcane Throne.

Characters & Classes

Six Paths.
One Destiny.

Choose your champion from six distinct classes — Archmage, Shadow Rogue, Iron Paladin, Storm Caller, Bone Weaver, or Wild Shaman — each with a unique deck of abilities, a signature spell, and a secret agenda.

World & Lore

The Labyrinth was not built — it grew. Scholars believe it is a living entity, reshaping its corridors each night to lure new prey. Those who enter seeking power rarely return unchanged, and some never return at all. Only the Arcane Throne knows the truth.
